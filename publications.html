Publications

This is a list of my publications and competition projects. I'm now working in the Institute of Advanced Communications, advised by Prof. Lingyang Song.
My research interests lie wildly in wireless networks and mobile computing. I'm also exploring unique intersections between machine learning and wireless networks (see our recent paper in ICC).
