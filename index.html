Selected Research Projects

This is a list of my research projects.

De Novo DNA Sequence Assembly from Barcoded Reads [Poster]
Fei Xia, Ilan Shomorony, David Tse.

We introduce necessary and sufficient conditions for a given repeat in the genome to be resolved by a barcoded fragment, which translates into minimum coverage requirements for perfect assembly as a function of the sequence repeat statistics. Based on these theoretical insights, we develop a new algorithm to perform de novo assembly from barcoded short reads. The algorithm takes a two-step approach. First, it uses the raw reads with no barcode information to construct a reliable de Bruijn graph. Then, the barcodes are mapped onto this graph and used to resolve repeats, leading to an increase in the resulting contig lengths. We quantify the impact of barcodes by studying the de Bruijn graph degree profile, N50, and maximum contig lengths before and after the repeat resolution step. Experiments on synthetic datasets and human genome datasets show the effectiveness of our algorithm.

Continuous Time Information Network Mining for Diffusion Cascades. [Paper]
Fei Xia, Yu Xia, Jie Tang.

We formally define the indirect influence and structural influence and consider these two effects when doing the network mining in our model ConInfMine. Furthermore, we use the network backbone as a input of our modelling. We perform some theoretical analysis on the convexity of the problem and convergence of the training procedure. Finally, experiments on both synthetic and real-world datasets demonstrate the effectiveness of our algorithm.

    • Softwares

    It has been some ten years since I took up coding. I like to do some software engineering in my spare time. Below is a list of softwares that I built or contributed to recently, either for research projects or for fun.
    Computational Biology

    10X-Bandage [Github]
    A hackish augmented version of Bandage that supports visualization of 10X barcoded reads on assembly graphs.

    A Pacbio Assembler [Available for collaborators]
    Joint work with Ilan Shomorony, Govinda Kamath, Thomas Courtade and David Tse. This is a de novo assembler for PacBio and other SMRT sequencing technologies. (currently at research prototype stage) We use a pipeline that is significantly different from HGAP and Falcon. We are able to generate finished assembly for E.Coli with 99.9% accuracy now (without polishing).

    Robots and Computer Vision

    Software for a robot that searches a room for human and do face recognition for ECE 4580 in GT. It is compatible with ROS framework.

    FaceRec/EmotionRec [Github] [Demo]
    A pipeline that does face detection, face alignment and face recognition. It has been used in RoboCup@Home 2014 in Brazil. And I added emotion recognition to it for a course project.
