About me

Hello, I'm Yuzhe Yang.

I am currently a senior undergraduate student in the School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at Peking University.

I worked in Institute of Advanced Communications, advised by Prof. Lingyang Song since 2016.

My research interests lie in wireless networking, mobile sensing and computing, machine learning and internet of things. I'm also interested in optimization theory, and the intersection between machine learning (deep learning) and wireless networks.
